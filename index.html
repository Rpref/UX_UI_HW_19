Hi! I'm Reagan Prefling.

A UX/UI Designer with a background in fashion design, sustainability, and a knack for information architecture.

For 10 years, I was a designer and product developer for Tory Burch. More recently, I have worked for small startups developing garments, data management systems, and operations.

Sustainable design is rooted in my long history of organizing information in a way that “makes sense”, and was brought to life in practice while developing 100% circular garments over the past three years. I have delighted both customers and stakeholders by creating products with lasting power, and systems that increase clarity and efficiency across functions.

As I strive to create positive impacts on the world, I wish to lend my expertise to purposeful companies by creating engaging experiences, improving internal systems, and beyond.

MY STRENGTHS

DEFINING PROBLEMS

You can’t solve a problem until you know what it is. I get to the root of an issue using strong analytical skills to synthesize research findings, and ideate on solutions that will work.

INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE

You can't find what you can’t see. I take a logical approach to information architecture, grouping site pages or arranging page hierarchy in a way that is intuitive and requires little thought for the user.

PROTOTYPING

Detail oriented by nature, prototyping comes naturally to me. Starting with the home page, I map out features needed to create a seamless user experience that both delights and makes sense.
